About our platform

Our support & recycling journey

  1. 01.

    Request IT help or e-waste pickup

    Tell us what is wrong or what you want recycled — phones, PCs, batteries, cables — and where you are in Uganda. One form for support, another for collection.

  2. 02.

    We triage and set a time with you

    We review your ticket, match you with an engineer for remote help, or confirm a pickup slot. You get clear next steps — no guesswork.

  3. 03.

    Resolve, recycle, and see your impact

    Fix issues with guidance, wipe data safely, or hand devices in for responsible processing. Follow requests and personal impact in your dashboard.
