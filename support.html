FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers before you submit a request.

My battery drains fast. What can I try first? + −
Go to Settings → Battery, disable background activity for heavy apps, reduce brightness, and restart the device.
My phone is slow. How do I speed it up? + −
Clear storage/cache, uninstall unused apps, update the OS, and restart. If storage is almost full, performance will drop.
Wi‑Fi isn’t connecting. What should I check? + −
Forget the network and reconnect. If it still fails, restart your router and reset network settings on the device.
Apps keep crashing. What should I do? + −
Update the app. If it still crashes, clear its cache/data, then reinstall. If multiple apps crash, update the OS.
